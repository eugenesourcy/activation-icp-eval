What This Means (Plain Language)

1) ICP Clarity Improved

The bot now distinguishes much better between viable leads (keep and advance), non-ICP leads (exit with specific doors), and handoff cases (calls, complex projects). Earlier versions confused these paths.

2) Conversation Efficiency Improved

v7 is much closer to WhatsApp-native behavior: short messages, one question, direct value first. This reduced dead turns and improved confidence in early lead engagement.

3) Remaining Risk Is Coverage

v7 core personas are strong, but adversarial personas from v4 were not re-run on v7. The next important step is re-running those skeptical buyer profiles to confirm the current prompt is robust.
